Kakum National Park, Ghana


Kakum National Park, located in the coastal environs of the Central Region of Ghana, covers an area of 375 square kilometres (145 sq mi).
Established in 1931 as a reserve, it was gazetted as a national park only in 1992
after an initial survey of avifauna was conducted. The area is covered with tropical forest.
